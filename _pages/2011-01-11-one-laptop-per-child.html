During the fall of 2010 I spent three months as a volunteer intern in rural Perú. My main goal was to educate teachers and students about how to better use their XO laptops. I ended up learning as much from them as I think they did from me.
While in Perú I developed a series of workshops to be used while teaching the kids. You can catch up on all the blog posts that I wrote while there. If you want a quick way to get an idea of what my experience was like, check out my final report. It is a six part series.
